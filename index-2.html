The Problem

Companies spend millions on cyber. Nobody knows if it's working.

Existing tools are siloed. Security teams track threats. Finance teams track spend. Nobody connects them. CyFinex does.

01
Are we overspending or underspending on cyber?
Most boards have no benchmark. They approve budgets blind, with no reference to actual risk exposure or peer spend.
02
What risk reduction are we actually buying?
Vendors claim risk reduction. Nobody measures it in financial terms. CISOs can't prove ROI to the CFO.
03
What would a breach cost us today?
Scenario modelling exists in isolation. It's never reconciled against live spend, insurance coverage, or control effectiveness.
04
Which vendors are delivering ROI?
Security tools proliferate. Budgets balloon. But there's no financial ledger that shows which tools reduce risk per dollar spent.
05
Is our insurance coverage sufficient?
Most companies discover insurance gaps after a breach. CyFinex surfaces coverage shortfalls in real time, before they become losses.
06
How should the board evaluate cyber budgets?
Boards get technical briefings. What they need is a financial view — spend, exposure, and efficiency — in a language they understand.
